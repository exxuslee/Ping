Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This application is designed to check website availability and measure response time. We take your privacy seriously and collect only the minimum amount of information necessary for the app to function.

2. Data We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The only information used by the app is the website addresses you manually enter and the technical ping results (response time, availability status). This data:

  • contains no personal information;
  • is not sent to the developer or third parties;
  • is used only locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

The app does not store any data remotely. Ping results exist only during app usage and may be temporarily stored in your device's memory.

4. Data Sharing

The app does not share any information with third parties. No analytics, tracking, or statistics collection is implemented.

5. Permissions

The app does not request access to personal data, files, camera, microphone, location, or any sensitive resources.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Any changes take effect immediately upon being posted on this page.
